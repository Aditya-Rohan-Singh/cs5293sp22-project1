TO: All OU Students

Interested in understanding how sleep may be affecting your mental health, especially anxiety and depression?

Abigail Sandoval, Cassi Pantalia, Cindy Saldivar, Hannah Griffin, and Preston Parks are looking for volunteers for their HES capstone study ea examining how sleep may be related to anxiety and depression.

Who is eligible?

Participants aged 18 to 30 who are undergraduate students at the University of Oklahoma.

What does testing involve?

Testing involves 
a single online questionnaire regarding your sleep and mental health within the past month. The questionnaire should take no more than 10 minutes to complete.
